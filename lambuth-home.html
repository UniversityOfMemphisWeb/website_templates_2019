Nationally distinguished. Locally yours.

With all the power of a major research university behind it, The University of Memphis Lambuth Campus brings visionary learning to West Tennessee. We give area residents a campus in their own backyard, making it very convenient to get a world class education close to home. And the Lambuth setting is just what you might expect in this friendly cityâ€”a 57-acre, tree-filled, tranquil campus with stately historic buildings. No vast parking lots. No high-rise buildings. You are simply surrounded by beauty and a genuine love for learning.

Lambuth Academy

The University of Memphis Lambuth Campus is proud to announce the formation of the Lambuth Academy. The Lambuth Academy offers dual enrollment opportunities for area high school students and home schooled students in the afternoons and evenings.
